Who We Are

Best Choice Dishes is an online and mobile food ordering and delivery marketplace dedicated to connecting diners with local restaurants in Lagos Island.

Our Mission

Our purpose is to connect diners and restaurants, to achieve this, we strive to create a workplace that reflects the diversity of our customers and the communities we serve. When you join our team, you become part of a values-driven community that works together to innovate, solve problems, take risks, grow, work hard and have a ton of fun in the process.

Our Goals

The main goal of Best Choice Dishes is to make food ordering and delivery easy. They are basically connecting the food joints in the city with customers to make online food ordering an option for users.

Our Services

Best Choice Dishes helps to find and order food from a wide array of restaurants within Lagos Island, with a single tap on their mobile phones, personal computers, and laptops.

Our Structure

Our fast-growing operations department includes: Director, Products manager, Product designers, Engineer, Product officer, to ensure consistent execution of day-to-day operations of their respective vertical, focusing primarily on the marketplace.
